Project description

The collaboration with Smuff started in the founding phase. The business idea for an online gadget store was ready and Smuff needed someone to build a web product around.

During years and iterations we have managed to build a custom e-commerce solution with a high conversion rate design and an automatic content distribution platform—measurable and optimizable—to aid sales and marketing.

Today Smuff is local market leader with 44% of returning customers.
