Project details

Anvelocom started as a Wordpress blog to research the market for auto off-road accessories and tuning equipment.

After initial success we've created a responsive e-commerce design, together with a strong visual identity replacing the Wordpress theme.

A technical challenge was to create dynamic filters for products which work with any number of categories and combinations.
